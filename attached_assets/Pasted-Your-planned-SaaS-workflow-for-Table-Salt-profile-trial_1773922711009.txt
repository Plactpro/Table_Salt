## Optimized SaaS Workflow
Use this sequence to minimize drop-offs and automate everything:

1. **User Registration/Profile Creation**: Simple email/password or Google/Apple signup → Instant basic profile (restaurant name, outlet count). No payment yet. [reddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/SaaS/comments/10jnl1f/how_do_you_handle_the_payment_flow_sign_up_for/)
2. **Trial Auto-Start**: Immediately activate 14-day free trial (no card required, opt-in model). Show "Trial Active: Add outlets & test POS/KDS now!" to drive quick value. [ordwaylabs](https://ordwaylabs.com/resources/white-papers/how-saas-free-trials-work/)
3. **Plan Selection Prompt**: After key activation (e.g., add first outlet or send test order), soft upsell: "Upgrade to Basic/Pro for unlimited users/multi-outlet". Offer tiers (e.g., Basic $49/mo, Pro $99/mo). [docs.stripe](https://docs.stripe.com/get-started/use-cases/saas-subscriptions)
4. **Payment & Subscription**: Integrate Stripe Checkout (one-click). On success, activate plan instantly (update user role, unlock features like advanced KDS/inventory). [freecodecamp](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/how-to-build-a-stripe-billing-onboarding-flow-for-your-saas-using-nodejs-and-react/)
5. **Onboarding & Usage**: Guided tour: "Add staff → Set up KDS → Process first order". Email reminders at Day 7/10/13 (value recaps, upgrade nudges). Trial ends → downgrade to freemium or prompt upgrade. [dansiepen](https://www.dansiepen.io/growth-checklists/saas-email-trial-period-tactics)

## Key Best Practices
- **Opt-in Trial**: No upfront card = higher signups (20-30% more); soft paywall after value shown. [ordwaylabs](https://ordwaylabs.com/resources/white-papers/how-saas-free-trials-work/)
- **Stripe Integration**: Handles trials, recurring billing, dunning (failed payments), multi-currency (UAE/India). [docs.stripe](https://docs.stripe.com/get-started/use-cases/saas-subscriptions)
- **Metrics to Track**: Trial-to-paid conversion (>15%), activation rate (e.g., outlets added Day 1), churn reason. [themeineed](https://themeineed.com/blog/saas-onboarding-flows-convert-trials-paying-customers)
- **Edge Cases**: Pause trial on inactivity; easy cancel → freemium; annual discount for Pro.
