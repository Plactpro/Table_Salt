**Restaurant Internal Audits Scheduling Module for Table Salt**

Create a comprehensive **Internal Audit Scheduling system** integrated into the **Table Salt** restaurant management application. This module enables restaurants to conduct regular self-audits for operational compliance, food safety, financial accuracy, and staff performance across daily, weekly, monthly, and quarterly cycles.

***

### **2. Daily Audit Checklists**

**Kitchen Operations Audit (Daily - 14:00):**
```
FOOD SAFETY (Critical)
☐ All fridges <5°C / freezers <-18°C (temp log check)
☐ Sanitizer buckets 100-200ppm (test strips)
☐ Cross-contamination prevention (color-coded boards)
☐ Date labels on all prepped items (<48hrs)
☐ Raw meat stored below ready-to-eat foods

OPERATIONS (High Priority)
☐ All equipment clean & functional
☐ Gas/electrical safety check (no exposed wires)
☐ Fire exits clear (30-second check)
☐ Pest control bait stations full
☐ Handwashing stations stocked
```

**Front of House Audit (Daily - 16:00):**
```
CUSTOMER SAFETY
☐ Restroom cleanliness (5-point check)
☐ Floor hazards (wet floor signs up)
☐ Table spacing compliant (fire code)
☐ Emergency lighting functional

SERVICE QUALITY
☐ Uniforms clean & name badges
☐ POS balanced vs cash drawer
☐ Menu prices match POS system
☐ Customer feedback collected (min 5/day)
```

### **3. Weekly Audit Checklists**

**Financial Audit (Monday 10:00):**
```
CASH HANDLING
☐ Cash reconciliation vs POS (3-day match)
☐ Voided transactions reviewed (<2% of sales)
☐ Safe combination changed monthly
☐ Petty cash balanced (<$50 variance allowed)
☐ Credit card receipts match POS

INVENTORY AUDIT
☐ Weekly stocktake vs PAR levels (80% accuracy)
☐ Liquor/beer stock rotation check (FIFO)
☐ Expiry date scan (high-value items)
☐ Supplier invoice vs delivery match
```

**Staff & Training Audit (Friday 11:00):**
```
☐ Food safety certification current (100% staff)
☐ Shift schedules posted (48hr advance)
☐ Training logs complete (new hires)
☐ Disciplinary records reviewed
☐ Uniform issue log updated
```

### **6. Audit Assignment & Completion**

**Role-Based Assignment:**
```
Manager: Full audit authority + approval
Supervisor: Daily checklists + photo evidence
Staff: Individual task completion
Auditor: External/3rd party access (Enterprise)
```

**Completion Requirements:**
```
☐ All checklist items marked (photo proof required)
☐ Supervisor/manager signature (digital)
☐ Corrective actions logged (if issues found)
☐ Follow-up verification (critical items)
```

### **7. Smart Audit Features**

**Risk-Based Prioritization:**
```
Critical (Red): Food safety, fire safety, cash handling
High (Orange): Staff compliance, inventory accuracy  
Medium (Yellow): Facilities, training records
Low (Green): Documentation, uniforms
```

**Auto-Scheduling:**
```
Daily audits: Operating days only
Weekly audits: Monday/Friday only  
Monthly audits: 1st of month
Quarterly: 90/60/30 days before health inspection
```

**Predictive Alerts:**
```
"Fridge temp failures 3x last week → Priority audit"
"Cash variance >2% → Immediate financial audit"
"New staff hired → Training compliance audit"
```
